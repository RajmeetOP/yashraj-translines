Business Information

Yashraj Translines, established in 1998, is a leading name in India's transport and logistics industry, proudly serving clients for over 27 years. Based in Mumbai, we are recognized for our commitment to punctuality, professionalism, and customer satisfaction. As experienced Fleet Owners and Transport Contractors, we specialize in delivering high-quality logistics solutions tailored to modern business needs.

Our core area of expertise lies in 40-feet container transportation, particularly for import and export consignments via JNPT Port — India's premier shipping gateway. Over the decades, we've developed a deep operational understanding of port handling, customs formalities, and reliable freight coordination. This enables us to provide end-to-end logistics support with unmatched efficiency.

With more than two decades of industry experience, we've earned the trust of renowned corporate clients, including Mahindra & Mahindra Ltd., CEAT Tyres Ltd., Ultra Laboratories Pvt Ltd., and Mangatram Clearing and Forwarding Agent Pvt Ltd.. Their continued association is a testament to the quality, consistency, and care that define our services.

At Yashraj Translines, we take pride in operating a well-maintained fleet, managed by skilled drivers and supported by a logistics team that values timeliness, cargo safety, and transparency. Whether handling regular dispatches or urgent consignments, we ensure your goods are delivered securely and on schedule—every time.

With a strong foundation built over 27+ years, Yashraj Translines continues to move forward — powering businesses, supporting supply chains, and setting standards in Indian logistics.
